High Peak Brewing Company

Alex Rios, Brewer

Fascinated with the science and art behind brewing, Alex pursued homebrewing as a hobby. After much reading and YouTube videos later, Alex finally took his first steps with a gallon starter kit to make a Pale Ale.


From there, Alex formulated more recipes and scaled up to 5 gallons. Later, Alex had the opportunity to work with a long-standing 50-bbl production brewery where he learned how to perform tasks in the following departments: Brew House, Cellar and Packaging. Compentencies include prepping raw material, use BeraBrew software to manage the brewing process; analyze yeast health, set up transfer lines, CIP & Post-Infect vessels and lines; clarifying beer, checking DO & carbonation specs before kegging beer.
